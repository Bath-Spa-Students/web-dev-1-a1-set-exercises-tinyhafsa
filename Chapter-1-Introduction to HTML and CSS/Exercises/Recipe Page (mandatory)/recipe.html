Chicken Pizza

Details Recipe Nutrition Side Dishes

Details

This Chicken Pizza comes together in about 30 minutes, and uses a bunch of grocery shortcuts, but still has big gourmet pizzeria flavor.

Servings: 4

Total time: 45 minutes

Prep time: 20 minutes

Bake time: 25 minutes

Recipe

For the dough

Ingredients:

  • 250g flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 1 cup water (for best results, use milk)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon Oregano leaves

Instructions:

  1. Add yeast and sugar to warm water and let it rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Mix the flour, oregano leaves, egg, salt, and oil.
  3. After the yeast has bloomed, add it little by little to the flour mixture and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Put the dough into a bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Store in a warm place for 2 hours to let the dough rise.
  5. When the size has doubled, take the ball out and beat it a few times before storing it away again (repeat this process at least two to three times for best results).

For the chicken

Ingredients:

  • 200g boneless chicken
  • 1 tablespoon garlic and ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon oil

Instructions:

  1. Chop the chicken into small pieces.
  2. Marinate the chicken with garlic and ginger paste, salt, red chilli powder and paprika. Let it rest for half and hour.
  3. Fry the chicken with the oil on medium heat until the color changes.
  4. Let the chicken cook on low heat for 5 to seven minutes.

For the pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon pizza sauce
  • 150g mozarella cheese
  • 1 cup cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 6-7 olives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven for 15 minutes at 280°C.
  2. Line a pizza tray with oil and stretch the dough on to it.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough. Add a layer of mozerella cheese.
  4. Add chicken and toppings. Cover with cheese again.
  5. Bake in oven for 20 to 25 minutes at 180°C. Make sure the cheese is melted and the dough is cooked through before taking it out of the oven.
