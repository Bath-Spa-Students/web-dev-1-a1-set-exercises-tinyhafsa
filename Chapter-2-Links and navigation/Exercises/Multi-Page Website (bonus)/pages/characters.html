Characters

Joseph Cooper

played by Matthew McConaughey

He is a trained NASA pilot with an engineering background, and the pilot of the interstellar expedition. Cooper has two children, Tom Cooper and Murphy Cooper.

❝ Once you're a parent, you're the ghost of your children's future. ❞

Murphy Cooper

played by Jessica Chastain

Joseph's daughter, who eventually becomes a NASA scientist. She discovers the gravity equation that helps save humanity.

❝ My dad thought I called it a ghost, because I was scared of it. But I was never scared of it. I called it a ghost, because it felt... it felt like a person; like it was trying to tell me something. ❞

Dr. Amelia Brand

played by Anne Hathaway

She is the chief scientist/biologist of the expedition being sent through the Wormhole attempting to relocate the human race. She is the daughter of NASA Director, Dr. John Brand. She has a niece and nephew from her brother.

❝ Love is the one thing we're capable of perceiving that transcends dimensions of time and space. ❞

Professor John Brand

played by Michael Caine

He was a scientist and director of the remnants of NASA, as well as the father of Amelia Brand. He asks former NASA pilot Joseph Cooper to join the expedition through the wormhole.

❝ I'm not afraid of death. I'm an old physicist - I'm afraid of time. ❞
